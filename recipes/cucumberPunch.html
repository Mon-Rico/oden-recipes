Cucumber Punch

Description

A refreshing and zesty cucumber punch that's perfect for summer gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 1 large cucumber, peeled and chopped
  • 1 cup of fresh mint leaves
  • 1/2 cup of lime juice
  • 1/4 cup of sugar
  • 4 cups of sparkling water
  • Ice cubes

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine the cucumber, mint leaves, lime juice, and sugar. Blend until smooth.
  2. Strain the mixture through a fine mesh sieve into a pitcher.
  3. Add the sparkling water and stir gently to combine.
  4. Serve over ice and garnish with additional mint leaves and cucumber slices if desired.
