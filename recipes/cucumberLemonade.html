Cucumber Lemonade

Description

A refreshing and tangy cucumber lemonade that's perfect for hot summer days.

Ingredients

  • 1 large cucumber
  • 1 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 4 cups water
  • Ice cubes
  • Mint leaves (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Peel and slice the cucumber.
  2. In a blender, combine cucumber slices, lemon juice, sugar, and 2 cups of water. Blend until smooth.
  3. Strain the mixture through a fine mesh sieve into a pitcher.
  4. Add the remaining 2 cups of water and stir well.
  5. Serve over ice and garnish with mint leaves.
