Alfredo Pasta

Description

A creamy and delicious Alfredo pasta that's perfect for a comforting meal.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Pour in the heavy cream and bring to a simmer. Stir in the Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth.
  4. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to coat in the Alfredo sauce. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve immediately, garnished with additional Parmesan cheese if desired.
